GOLF

Golf is a timeless sport that blends skill, strategy, and a deep connection with nature. Played on beautifully designed courses, it challenges players to navigate various terrains while aiming to sink the ball into a series of holes with as few strokes as possible. Known for its emphasis on precision and mental focus, golf is enjoyed by players of all ages and skill levels. Whether you're chasing a personal best or savoring a leisurely round with friends, golf offers a unique balance of competition and relaxation, making it a cherished pastime worldwide....

Q1. What is golf? ▼

Golf is a sport where players use clubs to hit a small ball into a series of holes on a course in as few strokes as possible. It’s played on outdoor courses with varying landscapes.

Q2.How many holes are there in a standard round of golf?▼

A standard round of golf consists of 18 holes. Some shorter courses may have only 9 holes.

Q3.What is par in golf?▼

Par is the number of strokes a skilled golfer is expected to take to complete a hole or a course. Each hole has a designated par, typically 3, 4, or 5.

Q4. What are the main types of golf clubs?▼

Golf clubs are categorized into drivers, woods, irons, wedges, and putters. Each is designed for specific types of shots.

Q5.What is a handicap in golf? ▼

A handicap is a numerical measure of a golfer's playing ability, allowing players of different skill levels to compete fairly. A lower handicap indicates a more skilled player.

Q6. What does a birdie mean in golf? ▼

A birdie is a score of one stroke under par on a hole. For example, completing a par-4 hole in 3 strokes is a birdie.
